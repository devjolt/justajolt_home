Leeds

I love Leeds because it has a Museum, an Art Gallery and good shopping.

This is the Brick Man. You can find him in Leeds Art Gallery. There is an art space inside where you can make your own artwork.

This is the Leeds City Museum. There are lots of interesting things inside. For example, this mummy of an Egyptian priest.

There are lots of good shops in Leeds like the Lindt shop where there are all sorts of chocolate flavors such as mint, strawberries and cream, coconut and even green tea!

This is the Trinity shopping Center where you can find the Lindt shop.
